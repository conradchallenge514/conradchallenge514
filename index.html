Introduction

Many customers would like to have data and recommendations on when network outages occur in their area. This may prevent issues such as data loss from occuring. This can include customers such as high frequency trading firms or cloud infrastructure providers. Using a proprietary algorithm and localized training data, our product can generate predictions on when a network outage may occur and send alerts to the customer.
